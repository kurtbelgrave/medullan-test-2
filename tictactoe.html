This is an implementation of the very well-known game, Tic-Tac-Toe, written entirely from scratch by Kofi Doku Atuah for the Medullan recruitment's 2nd test.

To play, first select whether you want to play versus the AI, or you want to play a two-player game. Then choose your desired difficulty. You can change these anytime by selecting new options and clicking reset.

To reset the board after a game is done, click reset.
